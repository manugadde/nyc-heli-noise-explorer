Feature summary

  • Demographics mode: Enable the Demographics chip and move the pointer on the map to compute 0.5-mile proximity totals for population, income, and complaints.
  • Complaints controls: The Complaints chip toggles complaint points on the map. The table action opens complaint records constrained by active filters and map extent.
  • Day filtering: Click a cell in Flights by Day to filter tracks, complaints, and side-panel analytics to a single date.
  • Aircraft browser: Review ranked aircraft IDs for the active date.
  • Flight detail chart: Select an aircraft ID to open a time-based chart and highlight related tracks and nearby complaints.
